## Overview

This template outlines the standard Service Agreement that is automatically created when a client approves a talent's proposal for professional voice acting services. The actual agreement will be populated with specific project details, payment terms, and timelines from the approved proposal.

## Key Terms

### 1. Permission and License to Use Voice

In accordance with Vietnamese Intellectual Property Law (Luật Sở hữu trí tuệ Việt Nam), this agreement constitutes explicit permission and a signed usage contract for the use of the voice talent's voice.

**Required Elements (as per Vietnamese Law):**

- **Usage Conditions (Điều kiện sử dụng):** Clearly specified in this agreement
- **Scope of Usage (Phạm vi sử dụng):** Detailed scope of where and how the voice work may be used
- **Remuneration (Mức thù lao):** Explicitly stated service fee and payment terms

The client must have this signed agreement before using the voice talent's voice in any project or commercial context.

### 2. Service Description

- Professional voice acting services

- Revision rounds as specified in the agreement
- **Voice Usage Scope:** The delivered voice work may only be used within the scope explicitly stated in this agreement

- Automatic release upon delivery and approval, or after 5 days without complaints
- All remuneration terms are clearly stipulated as required by Vietnamese law

### 4. Intellectual Property Rights (Quyền Sở hữu Trí tuệ)

**Under Vietnamese Intellectual Property Law:**

- **Work-for-Hire (Làm thuê):** Upon delivery, all intellectual property rights in the delivered voice work product automatically become the exclusive property of the client
- **Personal Rights (Quyền nhân thân):** The voice talent retains personal rights over their voice, and the client must respect these rights as stipulated in this agreement
- **Usage Limitations:** The client may not modify, edit, or use the delivered work beyond the agreed scope without explicit permission
- Talent retains no commercial rights, title, or interest in the delivered voice work product
- Portfolio usage rights subject to client approval and confidentiality terms

**Rights Protection:**

- If the client violates usage scope or conditions, the talent has the right to:
  - Request immediate cessation of unauthorized use
  - Request compensation for damages
  - Pursue legal action under Vietnamese law
