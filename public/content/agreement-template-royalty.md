# Royalty Agreement Template

**Agreement Type:** AI Voice Synthesis Licensing

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Overview

This template outlines the standard Royalty Agreement that is automatically created when a client approves a talent's proposal for AI voice usage. The actual agreement will be populated with specific project details, royalty rates, and licensing terms from the approved proposal.

## Key Terms

### 1. Permission and License to Use Voice (Giấy phép Sử dụng)

In accordance with Vietnamese Intellectual Property Law (Luật Sở hữu trí tuệ Việt Nam), this agreement constitutes explicit permission and a signed usage contract for the use of the voice talent's voice for AI synthesis purposes.

**Required Elements (as per Vietnamese Law):**

- **Usage Conditions (Điều kiện sử dụng):** Clearly specified in this agreement
- **Scope of Usage (Phạm vi sử dụng):** Detailed scope of where and how the voice may be used for AI synthesis
- **Remuneration (Mức thù lao):** Explicitly stated royalty rate and payment structure

The client must have this signed agreement before using the voice talent's voice for any AI synthesis or commercial purpose.

### 2. Voice Licensing (Cấp phép Giọng nói)

- **Explicit Permission:** This agreement grants the client explicit permission to use the talent's voice for AI synthesis, as required by Vietnamese law
- **License Terms:** License terms and scope are clearly specified in this agreement
- **Voice Ownership (Quyền sở hữu):** Talent retains ownership of their voice and personal rights (quyền nhân thân)
- **License Limitations:** The client's license is limited to the scope explicitly stated in this agreement

### 3. Royalty Payments (Thanh toán Bản quyền)

- **Royalty Rate (Mức thù lao bản quyền):** As agreed and clearly stated in the proposal - [Amount in agreement]
- **Payment Structure:** Detailed payment structure for AI voice usage as stipulated in this agreement
- **Remuneration Terms:** All royalty and remuneration terms are explicitly documented as required by Vietnamese law
- Payment secured in escrow upon agreement creation
- Automatic release upon delivery and approval, or after 5 days without complaints

### 4. Intellectual Property Rights (Quyền Sở hữu Trí tuệ)

**Under Vietnamese Intellectual Property Law:**

- **Voice Ownership (Quyền sở hữu giọng nói):** Talent retains full ownership of their voice
- **Personal Rights (Quyền nhân thân):** Talent retains personal rights over their voice, including the right to control how and where it is used
- **License Grant (Cấp phép):** Client receives a license to use voice for AI-generated content within the explicitly stated scope
- **Royalty Obligation:** Client must pay royalties as specified in this agreement for usage of the voice

**Rights Protection:**

- If the client violates license scope, usage conditions, or fails to pay royalties:
  - Talent has the right to request immediate cessation of unauthorized use
  - Talent has the right to request compensation for damages and unpaid royalties
  - Talent may pursue legal action under Vietnamese law
  - Client may not use the voice beyond the agreed scope without additional permission and compensation

### 6. Delivery & Approval

- Deliverables must meet specifications in the agreement
- Client has 5 days from delivery to raise disputes
- Automatic acceptance if no complaints within 5 days

### 7. Escrow Protection

- Payment held securely by VoiceTalents.AI
- Released automatically upon approval or after 5-day dispute window

### 8. Documentation and Record Keeping

As required for legal compliance in Vietnam:

- This digital agreement serves as the official usage contract (hợp đồng sử dụng) and license agreement
- All terms, conditions, scope, royalty rates, and remuneration are documented in this agreement
- Both parties must retain copies for legal purposes and potential disputes

- Regular reporting of usage and royalty calculations may be required as specified in the agreement
